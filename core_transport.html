The core_transport IDS is a container object that has a single data object, a double(:) with all the timeslice values for scalar data. The core_transport IDS also holds five container objects with data, or meta data, or other containers: ids_properties, code, vacuum_toroidal_field, model and profiles_1d, respectively. The containver profiles_1d holds the three containers grid_d, grid_v and grid_flux. The model container has two containers: identifier and profiles_1d. The latter in turn has containers total_ion_energy, momentum_tor, electrons, ion(nion) and neutral(nneut). electrons has the two containers particles and energy. The ion and neutral containers have sub containers element, particles, energy and state, where the latter itself has sub containers particles and energy, and neutral_type (ion sub container state only). The core_transport IDS has a total of 28 container objects with 320 data (or meta data) objects.
